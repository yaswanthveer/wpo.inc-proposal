Our Heritage

K. S. Aiyar & Co.

A legacy of trust, commercial education, and integrity in Indian finance.

Founded by **Shri Kalyan Subramani Aiyar** (1859–1940), who is widely regarded as the **"Father of the Accountancy Profession in India"**, K.S. Aiyar & Co. has shaped Indian professional standards for over 130 years.

1897
The Inception
Established professional practice in Calicut as the first Indian-led accounting firm.
1900
Bombay Expansion
Moved the headquarters to Bombay, establishing a national center of integrity.
1912
Commerce Degrees
Established India's first Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) program at Bombay University.
1913
Sydenham College
Founded Sydenham College, India's first dedicated college of commerce.
The Challenge

The Problem We Face Every Audit Season

Our teams spend too much time documenting and too little time auditing.

Process Inefficiencies
  • Preparing working papers **manually** for every audit area.
  • **Repeating similar documentation** across multiple client folders.
  • Evidence scattered across **emails, chats, and personal drives**.
  • Increased documentation expectations under Standards on Auditing.
Documentation Bottleneck

The modern challenge is no longer performing audit procedures.

The bottleneck lies in **documenting them efficiently, consistently, and completely** under severe timeline pressures.

Strategic Direction

Why Change is Necessary

The auditing profession is evolving rapidly.

Market Pressures
  • Clients demand **faster turnaround times** and instant insights.
  • Regulatory and documentation requirements continue to expand.
  • Professional bodies urge immediate **technology adoption**.
